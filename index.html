About

Who we are and why we exist

Our Mission

A spiritual community dedicated to upliftment, unity, and cultural celebration through reggae music, positive energy, and healthy living.

Our Vision

A thriving community where culture, wellness, and spiritual awareness empower people to live healthier, more connected, and more purposeful lives.

Our Values

Unity • Cultural pride • Wellness • Love & respect • Community service • Positive energy

“We’re building a safe, uplifting space where people can connect, grow, celebrate culture, and support one another.”

— Church of I-ration

Programs & Gatherings

What we do (and what we’re growing)

Cultural Gatherings

Music-centered events, community fellowship, and celebrations that honor heritage and bring people together.

Wellness & Healthy Living

Education and activities that support healthier lifestyles, stress reduction, and holistic well-being.

Youth & Creative Expression

Mentorship, learning opportunities, and creative outlets that inspire leadership, confidence, and growth.
